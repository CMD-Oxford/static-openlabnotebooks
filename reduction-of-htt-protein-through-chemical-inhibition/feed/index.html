However, by looking at the reduction in HTT protein levels, LLY283 seems to be more potent than GSK591 and was able to induce a greater downregulation of HTT protein levels at the same concentration asGSK591. This might be due to the difference in the way GSK591 and LLY283 work as they inhibit PRMT5 through different mechanisms, or it might simply be due to differences in their chemical properties, which affect how easily they can enter the cells.
